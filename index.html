How it works

Four steps to a perfect night

From discovery to morning-after bragging rights — HappyMaps has your whole night covered.

1

See Tonight's Hotspots

The Tonight feed shows which bars are trending right now, how many students are going, and what's #1 right now in your city. Know before you go.

2

See Where Your Friends Are Going

Friends Going Out Tonight shows you exactly where your crew is headed. See their bar picks in real time and coordinate without a single text.

3

Plan With Your Group

Create a private Group, invite your crew, and put the bar decision to a vote. When everyone's in, hit Let's Roll.

4

Rate Every Bar You Visit

Rate bars across 7 categories — Drinks, Music, Crowd, Value, Happy Hour, Venue, and Dancing. Build your personal nightlife record and share your ratings as a card.

Features

Everything for a great night out

Built for the way college students actually plan their nights — social, spontaneous, and always moving.

Tonight Feed & Trending

See which bars are picking up, getting busy, or on fire right now. The Tonight feed ranks bars by live student activity so you always know where the energy is.

College Student Breakdown

See exactly how many students from your school are planning to go to each bar tonight. A private signal just for your campus community.

Groups & Voting

Create a private group with your crew, see where each member is going tonight, and put the bar decision to a democratic vote. The organizer approves every member.

Live Map View

Explore bars across your city on a live map. Pins are color-coded by activity level — Quiet, Picking Up, Busy, or Most Popular Tonight.

Subcategory Ratings

Rate every bar across 7 categories and build your personal nightlife record. See your top bar for Drinks, Music, Crowd, and more — and share your ratings as a beautiful card.

Your Nightlife Profile

Track your nights out, bars visited, and crawls. See tonight's plan right on your profile, and share your top picks with friends.
