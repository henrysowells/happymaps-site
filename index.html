How it works

Four steps to a perfect night

From discovery to morning-after bragging rights — HappyMaps has your whole night covered.

1

Discover bars near you

Browse tonight's hottest spots in your city, sorted by crowd and vibe. Filter by neighborhood, price, or type.

2

Check crowd estimates

See how many people are going to each bar tonight in real time. Know before you go — no more arriving to an empty room.

3

Plan with friends

Create a private bar crawl, invite your crew, and lock in your route for the night. Keep everyone on the same page.

4

Rate & track your nights

Log every bar you've been to, leave ratings, and build a history of your nights out on a personal calendar.

Features

Everything for a great night out

Built for the way college students actually plan their nights — social, spontaneous, and always moving.

📍

Bar Discovery & Crowd Prediction

See what's popping tonight with real-time crowd estimates. The Tonight feed shows each bar's vibe, price range, and how many people are going — before you leave home.

🗺️

Interactive Map View

Explore bars across your city on a live map. See crowd counts pinned to each location so you can visualize where the energy is and plan your route.

🚶

Bar Crawl Planning

Build a custom bar crawl, invite friends, and keep the group moving together. Private and invite-only so your plans stay yours.

🎓

College Community Groups

Join your school's community with a verified college email. See what's hot on your campus tonight and connect with fellow students.

🙈

Anonymous Safety for Non-Friends

Crowd data is always aggregated — non-friends can never see who's going where. Your whereabouts are private to your friend circle.

⭐

Bar Ratings, Happy Hours & Deals

Rate every bar you visit (think Beli for nightlife). Discover happy hour deals and get direct links to Maps and bar websites so you're always informed.
