Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your data is removed within 30 days. If you can't access the app, email henry@happymapsapp.com from the address you signed up with and we'll handle it.

Which schools does HappyMaps work for?

You can pick your school during sign-up. We're live for UCLA, USC, LMU, and Pepperdine in Los Angeles, and Georgetown, GW, and American in DC. More on the way.

Who has to be 21+?

HappyMaps is for users 21 and older. We verify age at sign-up.

Who can see where I'm going tonight?

Only your friends and people in your groups. Your school sees aggregate counts ("12 UCLA students going") but never your name unless you're friends. You can stop showing as "going" any time by tapping the button again.

Is HappyMaps free?

Yes — and it stays free for students.
